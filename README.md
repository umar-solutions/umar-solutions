### Comparative Study of Optimization Methods for Neural Network Hyperparameter Tuning

My masterâ€™s thesis investigates the **trade-offs between global optimization methods and Bayesian optimization** for hyperparameter optimization in neural networks.

As model complexity increases, traditional tuning strategies face a fundamental tension between **computational cost** and **solution quality**. This research examines how different optimization paradigms behave under realistic evaluation budgets.

**Methods studied**
- Random Search (baseline)
- Differential Evolution (DE)
- CMA-ES
- Bayesian Optimization (Gaussian Processes)
- Bayesian Optimization (TPE / Optuna)

- Scalability in high-dimensional spaces
- Robustness to noise and mixed variable types
- Practical implications for real ML systems

- Practitioner-oriented guidance on method selection
